Frequently Asked Questions

What is TDEE?

Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It's the total calories you burn in a day based on your activity level.

How often should I recalculate?

Recalculate every time you lose or gain 5kg of body weight, as your metabolic needs change.

Is the calorie count exact?

It's a professional estimate. Individual genetics can vary by 10-15%, so use as a starting baseline.

Can I use this for weight loss?

Yes! For weight loss, subtract 500 calories from the TDEE shown above for a healthy calorie deficit.
