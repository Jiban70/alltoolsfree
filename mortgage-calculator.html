Global Mortgage Calculator for All Countries

Our Universal Mortgage Calculator works in every country worldwide. Whether you're buying property in the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, India, Nepal, or any other country, this tool calculates accurate monthly payments using the international standard formula:

EMI = [P × r × (1+r)^n] / [(1+r)^n - 1]
Where P = Principal loan amount, r = Monthly interest rate, n = Loan term in months

Why This Calculator Works Everywhere: Mortgage mathematics is universal. Whether you call it EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) in India and Nepal, "mortgage payment" in the USA and UK, or "home loan repayment" in Australia - the calculation remains the same. Only the currency symbol changes!

Local Considerations by Region:

  • USA/Canada: Typically 15-30 year fixed rates, property taxes separate
  • UK/Europe: Often includes arrangement fees, variable rates common
  • India/Nepal: EMI calculations include processing fees, shorter terms
  • Australia: Offset accounts common, interest-only periods available
