How to Create a Professional E-Signature

In 2026, signing PDFs and digital contracts is the standard. Using the AllToolsFree E-Signature Maker, you can draw your signature using a mouse, stylus, or your finger on touch devices. Our tool automatically exports your drawing as a 32-bit transparent PNG, meaning it will look natural when placed over the signature line of any document.

Why choose our Signature Tool?

  • Zero Tracking: Your biometric signature data never leaves your computer. It is processed locally in RAM.
  • Transparency: No white backgrounds. Your signature is "cut out" perfectly for professional overlaying.
  • Universal Compatibility: Use your saved PNG in Adobe Acrobat, DocuSign, Microsoft Word, or Google Docs.

Pro-Tip for Signing:

If you are using a mobile device, rotate your phone to Landscape Mode before signing to give yourself more horizontal space for a longer signature name.
