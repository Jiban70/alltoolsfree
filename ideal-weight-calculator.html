About Ideal Weight

Ideal Body Weight (IBW) is an estimate used by medical professionals for medication dosages and health assessments. Our calculator averages four scientific formulas for the most accurate result.

Frequently Asked

What's the best formula?

Most doctors prefer the Devine Formula, but we average all four for balanced results.

Does this account for muscle?

No. These formulas are height/gender based. Athletes may weigh more due to muscle mass.

Why include gender?

Men and women have different bone densities and muscle-to-fat ratios, affecting weight baselines.
